Pasta Alfredo

  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Cream
  • oil
  • Butter
  1. Put in a cooking pot water
  2. Heat the water until the water boil
  3. Put the pasta in the pot water and wait 15 minutes
  4. Remove the water from the pasta with a strainer
  5. Put in a cooking pot the butter and the oil
  6. Wait until the butter is hot
  7. Put the pasta on the cooking pot and add a little salt and the cream
  8. Move the cream well and all so the dish is finish
